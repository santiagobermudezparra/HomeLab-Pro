<objective>
Migrate both CNPG PostgreSQL cluster PVCs (linkding-postgres-1 and n8n-postgresql-cluster-1) from local-path to Longhorn using the CNPG bootstrap.recovery workflow.

Purpose: CNPG manages its own PVCs via volumeClaimTemplates — `storageClassName` cannot be changed on existing clusters. The migration requires deleting the CNPG Cluster object (which also deletes its PVC), then recreating the Cluster with `spec.storage.pvcTemplate.spec.storageClassName: longhorn` and `spec.bootstrap.recovery` pointing to a fresh R2 backup. CNPG handles data restoration automatically. After this plan, both PostgreSQL clusters will have their data on Longhorn with 2-node replication.

This is the highest-risk plan in Phase 7. Both linkding and n8n will be unavailable while their PostgreSQL clusters are being recreated and restored. Execute linkding first, verify fully, then execute n8n.

Output: Both `postgresql-cluster.yaml` files updated with `pvcTemplate.spec.storageClassName: longhorn`; both CNPG clusters healthy; both app PVCs on Longhorn; apps reconnected to their databases.
</objective>
