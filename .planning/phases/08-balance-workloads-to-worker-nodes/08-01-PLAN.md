<!-- Current cloudflared Deployment structure (from apps/staging/linkding/cloudflare.yaml) -->
<!-- Every active app follows this same pattern. -->

```yaml
apiVersion: apps/v1
kind: Deployment
metadata:
  name: cloudflared
spec:
  selector:
    matchLabels:
      app: cloudflared
  replicas: 2
  template:
    metadata:
      labels:
        app: cloudflared
    spec:
      containers:
      - name: cloudflared
        # ... (volumes, probes, args)
```

<!-- topologySpreadConstraints to add under spec.template.spec (NOT under spec): -->
```yaml
      topologySpreadConstraints:
        - maxSkew: 1
          topologyKey: kubernetes.io/hostname
          whenUnsatisfiable: DoNotSchedule
          labelSelector:
            matchLabels:
              app: cloudflared
```

<!-- IMPORTANT RULES:
  - topologyKey MUST be kubernetes.io/hostname (dynamic — works for any node count)
  - labelSelector MUST match the pod labels (app: cloudflared)
  - Do NOT use nodeName, nodeSelector with specific node names, or nodeAffinity with values: [worker-01, worker-02]
  - maxSkew: 1 means at most 1 pod difference between nodes
  - whenUnsatisfiable: DoNotSchedule means the scheduler will not schedule the pod if it cannot satisfy the constraint
-->
