<objective>
Disable Flannel CNI on the K3s cluster by editing the control-plane config and restarting K3s on all 3 nodes. Remove the flannel.1 interface on the control-plane. This opens the maintenance window — nodes will be in NotReady state with no CNI until Plan 02 installs Cilium.

Purpose: This is the prerequisite step for Cilium installation. K3s must be told to not manage networking before Cilium can take over. Without this, two CNIs fight over the same network interfaces.
Output: 3-node cluster with no active CNI (brief outage), ready to receive Cilium in Plan 02.
</objective>
